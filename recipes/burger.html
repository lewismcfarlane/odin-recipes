Burgers

Juicy beef patties, melted cheese, and fresh toppings, all nestled in a soft, toasted brioche bun - this classic burger recipe is pure comfort on a plate.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef, 80% lean, 20% fat
  • Brioche burger buns
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • American cheese slices
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Onions, sliced
  • Baconaise sauce (or your choice of sauce, any work!)

How to cook

  1. Prepare the Burger Patties
    • Divide the ground beef into equal portions, depending on the desired patty side (usually 1/4 to 1/3 pound per patty).
    • Gently shape each portion into a patty, being careful not to overwork the meat.
    • Season both sides of each patty with salt and black pepper.
  2. Preheat the Pan
    • Heat your pan over a medium-high heat stove.
  3. Grill the Burger Patties
    • Place the patties in the pan.
    • Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, adjust the cooking time to your preferred doneness.
    • When cooking the last side of your burger, in the last minute, add cheese to the burger to melt it.
  4. Toast the Burger Buns
    • While the patties are cooking, slice the brioche burger buns in half.
    • Place the buns in a separate heated pan for about 1-2 minutes, or until they are lightly toasted.
  5. Assemble the Burgers
    • Spread a layer of baconaise (or desired sauce) on the bottom half of each bun.
    • Place one lettuce leaf on top of the sauce.
    • Add a tomato slice, followed by sliced onion rings.
    • Place a cooked patty on top of the toppings.
    • Finish constructing the burger by placing the bun on top.
  6. Serve
    • Serve your classic beef burgers immediately while they are hot and juicy!

Now you're ready to enjoy delicious, homemade classic beef burgers with all the fixings!
