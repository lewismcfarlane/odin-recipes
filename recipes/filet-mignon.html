Filet Mignon

Filet Mignon: A masterpiece of culinary perfection, a tender steak seared to perfection and bursting with rich, mouthwatering flavour.

Ingredients

  • 2 Filet Mignon steaks (6-8 oz each)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Olive oil or clarified butter, for searing
  • Fresh herbs, thyme or rosemary
  • Garlic cloves

How to cook

  1. Preparation
    • Remove the filet mignon steaks from the fridge.
    • Let the steaks sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes before cooking.
  2. Season the steaks
    • Season both sides of each steak generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Preheat the oven
    • Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius. This step is essential for finishing the steaks after searing.
  4. Sear the steaks
    • Heat an oven-safe pan over high heat. Add a small amount of olive oil or clarified butter and swirl the pan to coat evenly.
    • When the pan is hot and the oil is shimmering, carefully place the seasoned filet mignon steaks in the pan.
    • Sear the steaks for about 2-3 minutes each side, or until a rich, golden-brown crust forms. For medium rare, the internal temperature should reach about 55-57 degrees Celsius. Adjust the cooking time based on your desired level of doneness.
    • During the last minute of searing, add the herbs and garlic for additional flavour.
  5. Finishing in the Oven
    • Once the steaks are seared to your liking, transfer the pan with the steaks to the preheated oven.
    • Roast the steaks in the oven for an additional 5 to 10 minutes, or until they reach your desired internal temperature. Use a meat thermometer to check the temperature.
  6. Rest the Steaks
    • Remove the pan from the oven, and transfer the steaks to a plate.
    • Cover the steaks loosely with aluminum foil and let them rest for about 5 minutes. This resting period allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a juicy and tender finish.
  7. Serve
    • After resting, plate the filet mignon steaks and serve them as desired. They pair wonderfully with side dishes such as mashed potatoes, steamed greens, creamy spinach or a simple salad.

This recipe will give you a tender, juicy filet mignon with a flavourful crust - perfect for a special meal or any occasion!
