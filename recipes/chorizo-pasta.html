Chorizo Pasta

A delectable fusion of smokey Spanish chorizo and creamy pasta, a harmonious blend of savoury flavours and indulgent textures that tantalise the taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Fusilli Pasta
  • Spanish chorizo sausage
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Red bell pepper
  • Canned diced tomatoes/tomato sauce
  • Smoked paprika, red pepper flakes and salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro
  • Grated cheese

How to cook

  1. Boil the Pasta
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
    • Add the fusilli pasta and cook according to package instructions until it's al dente.
    • Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. Prepare the Chorizo and Sauce
    • Heat a large frying pan over medium heat.
    • Add olive oil and then the Spanish chorizo.
    • Cook the chorizo until it's slightly crisped, and has released its flavourful oils.
    • Add diced onions and garlic to the pan and saute until they become translucent.
  3. Add Tomatoes and Seasoning
    • Pour in canned tomatoes or tomato sauce into the pan, stirring well.
    • Season the sauce with paprika, red pepper flakes, salt and black pepper.
    • Allow the sauce to simmer for a few minutes to meld the flavours.
  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce
    • Add the cooke fusilli pasta to the pan with the chorizo and the sauce.
    • Gently toss everything together to ensure the pasta is coated evenly with the sauce.
  5. Serve
    • Once the pasta is heated through and well-coated in sauce, it's ready to serve.
    • Garnish the dish with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro for a burst of freshness.
    • Add grated cheese to the dish to add extra richness.

The simple recipe yields a delicious and satisfying chorizo pasta that's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends and family!
